How does it work?

ADFGX Cloud Factoring Application was made to help the community come together to crack the ADFGX Cipher. With the Distributive Computing Power of the communities PC, we can Cloud Source a tremonous amount of resources together to find the best results for the cipher. The application uses English Ngrams to score each return from every key your PC tries. When the score is a decent score (-115 being a standard Egnlish Sentence), it is sent to the Server to be archived in the DB.

You will get to witness the community working together by visiting the "Top Page" in the application, which will contain the Best Results, Scores, and even the name of the person who happened to find that great result. Its like a leaderboard for finding the cipher answer! Will your PC be the lucky one to find the answer, and go down in history as the person who solved the ADFGX?
